We Were Grey

But now there is only darkness!

Robert Grimm
February 19, 2018

It is with inconsolable sadness and heartbreak that the royal household is announcing the passing of King Arthur. He was grey, standing between the candle and the star. Thanks to Him, we were grey, standing between the darkness and the light. But now there is only darkness!

We shall forever miss His meow, carefully and sparingly employed for altogether heightened effect and urgency. We were grey. But now there is only darkness!

We shall forever miss His affection for us subjects, whom He was always willing to grace with a rub or snuggle. His absence will be especially hard on feet and shoes, who benefited tremendously from His most generous adoration. We were grey. But now there is only darkness!

We shall forever miss lying down to sleep and feeling His lithe jump onto bed and pitter-patter across, His purr-some kneading of the comforter and curling up at our side, and finally His unwavering rest until the morning stirs. Without Him, only the emptiness of restless nights beckons. We were grey. But now there is only darkness!
